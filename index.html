Project Context

Our Mandate: Insulate ELEC-INDIA from Global Supply Shocks.

As a leading Indian electronics manufacturer, our growth is intrinsically linked to the stability of the global semiconductor supply chain. Leadership tasked our team, the Strategic Supply Chain Taskforce, to quantify our exposure to geopolitical disruptions and develop a robust, proactive framework to ensure production continuity.

Executive Briefing: Mainboard Chipset BCP

Situation Analysis

Baseline strategy faces a 78.5% stockout risk—a critical threat to production.

Recommended Mitigation

Dual-sourcing reduces risk to a manageable 14.2% for a marginal 8.1% cost increase.

Immediate Action Plan

  1. Approve the dual-sourcing strategy for the Mainboard Chipset.
  2. Initiate technical qualification and contracting with Intel Fab (USA).
  3. Procure a 60-day strategic buffer to ensure supply continuity.
